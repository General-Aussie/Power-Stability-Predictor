The Power Stability Predictor utilizes K-means clustering to identify stable and unstable clusters in the "P" and "Q" data. The cluster chart uses colors to represent the stability categories, with yellow indicating stability and purple indicating instability. The pie chart complements the cluster chart by providing a summary of the distribution of data points across these stability categories. Together, these visualizations aid in analyzing power stability patterns and assessing the prevalence of stable and unstable power behavior.
